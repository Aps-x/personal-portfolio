NOTICE: Originally designed for desktop not for websites. Chrome is recommended.

Controls

[Player 1]

[Ctrl] - Flap

[←] - Move left

[→] - Move right

[Player 2]

[Space] - Flap

[A] - Move left

[D] - Move right

Press [R] to reset.

Description

This was my fourth game programmed in Unity. This project took roughly 5 days to complete. It is a clone of the 1982 classic arcade title "Joust" by Midway. I originally intended to recreate the full game, however, I realized during development that the knight/buzzard AI was well beyond my current capability. On the other hand, I am rather happy with how the player movement turned out. This clone of Joust is meant as a learning exercise / for educational purposes.
