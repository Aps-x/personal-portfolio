Description

This was my fourth game programmed in Unity. This project took roughly 5 days to complete. It is a clone of the 1982 classic arcade title "Joust" by Midway. I originally intended to recreate the full game, however, I realized during development that the knight/buzzard AI was well beyond my current capability. On the other hand, I am rather happy with how the player movement turned out. I plan on revisiting this project once I have more experience with simpler AI first. This clone of Joust is meant as a learning exercise / for educational purposes.
